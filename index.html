"Discover the world in a cup."

Tea is a drink made by pouring hot water over the dried leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant. From that one plant, we get almost all the classic teas:

Green tea – Leaves are quickly heated so they stay green and taste fresh.

Black tea – leaves are fully oxidized, so they turn dark and taste stronger.

Oolong – partly oxidized, in between green and black.
